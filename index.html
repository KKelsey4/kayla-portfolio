About me

<----- Hover Over Me

Before I traded in the salty breeze for student life, I spent my twenties adventuring across the globe. From the Mediterranean to the Caribbean, even to the icy plains of Antarctica, I've seen (and photographed) it all. The world tour was epic, but I had a new desire to move back home to the motherland and earn myself a degree in Digital Design.

While the design world initially called my name, photography is my true passion. Balancing my studies with part-time work at The Digital Media Collective has given me valuable experience in understanding how visual storytelling can be used strategically.

Whether crafting digital experiences or weaving a story through a photograph, I aim to create something that sparks a daydream, ignites the imagination, and leaves a lasting impression. I'm still a work in progress, but I'm constantly learning, experimenting, and pushing myself to capture the magic.
